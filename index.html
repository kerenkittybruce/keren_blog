5 Easy Ways To Ground Yourself


It is common for us to experience stress when things don't go our way, and usually it builds up in such a way that we feel overwhelmed .
It is important for us to maintain a healthy positive attitude, mindfulness and resilience .


Below are some of the ways you can calm yourself down when you're feeling overwhelmed .

  • Perform various breathing exercises such as taking up to ten deep and slow breaths .

  • Always speak towards yourself in a positive, compassionate tone .

  • Pray, meditate and/or practise yoga to connect to your spiritual self .

  • Find your passion and transform it into a fun hobby, or even a source of income .

  • Spend time in positive environments with people who care about you .
